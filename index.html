Your data never leaves your browser. All processing happens locally.
  1. Go to Amazon.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Navigate to Account & Lists → Your Account.
  3. Scroll to "Privacy" and click "Request your data".
  4. Select "Your Orders" from the category dropdown and submit.
  5. Wait for an email from Amazon (usually within 1–3 days) with a download link.
  6. Download the .zip file and upload it here. That's it!
